Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Welcome to TripWander (the “App”). We highly value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Important Notice: TripWander follows a local-first data storage strategy. Except for transaction data required by the App Store, we do not upload your trip data to any server.

1. Information We Collect

TripWander is an offline utility app and follows the principle of data minimization:

  • Trip Data: All trips, notes, and budget records you create are stored entirely on your device (sandbox). We do not access, view, or synchronize this data.
  • Photos and Media: Photos you choose to import are stored locally on your device and are only used to generate shared images or PDF files.

2. System Permissions

To provide full functionality, the App may request the following permissions. You may revoke them at any time in system settings, which may limit certain features:

  • Photos Permission: Used to select photos from your library or save generated images/PDFs. We only access items you explicitly choose.
  • Camera Permission: Used to take photos and add them to your trips (if applicable).
  • Notifications: Used solely for local trip reminders triggered on your device.

3. Third-Party Services and In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium templates:

  • Transaction Data: All payments are processed by Apple App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.
  • Purchase Records: We receive anonymous transaction receipts from Apple solely to verify premium feature access.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. Data security therefore depends on your device security.

Backups: We recommend using iCloud or iTunes device backups, or exporting your trip data manually. If you uninstall the App, all data stored in the app sandbox will be permanently deleted by iOS and cannot be recovered.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be announced within the App.
